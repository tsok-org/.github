The Source of Knowledge

An AI Lab focused on creating software solutions for AI Agents, specializing in human and AI agent collaboration. We advance the field through practical software solutions, research, and open-source contributions.

We are building the infrastructure and tools that power the next generation of AI agents. Our work spans from foundational research in multi-agent coordination to production-ready frameworks that teams use daily to deploy intelligent systems. We focus on advancing open protocols like MCP (Model Context Protocol) and A2A (Agent2Agent), which enable standardized communication and collaboration within agentic ecosystems.

Looking ahead, we're expanding our research into adaptive learning systems and developing new standards for agent interoperability. By supporting the developer ecosystem with tools, libraries, and best practices, we aim to accelerate the adoption of agentic systems. We believe the future lies in transparent, reliable AI systems that augment human capabilities while remaining understandable and trustworthy.

Explore our papers, projects, open source contributions, and meet our team.
